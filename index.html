Mary Heisey

Mary Heisey is a contributor of Red Rover Camping. A North Carolina transplant from South Central Pennsylvania, she has an undying love for mountains and pretzels, two things one can never get enough of. Within one month, she traveled to the Philippines and immediately headed Southwest upon arrival back to the States for a half-cross-country road trip in a 2004 Scion xB. One of her biggest regrets in life was "getting poison oak once."
